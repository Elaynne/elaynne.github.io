About the game

This game uses RPG as a way to bring fun in math class by unifying the educational context to the ludic context. It also uses voice commands through isolated word recognition technology through Convolutional Neural Networks.

Story: You were in class and you fell asleep. When he roped, he was in a world destroyed by dragons and all you want is to go home. For this you will have to defeat the Thyme dragon controlled by the Master of the Calculations and collect the stones of intelligence in each world.
